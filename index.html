WELCOME to my 'everything vanlife' website that centres around my little microcosm within the community....

Here, you'll (hopefully) find the answers to the vanlife universes deepest darkest questions: van build how-to’s, roadtrip suggestions, adventure videos, vlogs, blogs, articles, adventures, product recommendations and information on van rentals. You can even book to rent my van out if you want to taste the sweet sweet nectar of #vanlife before committing to building or buying your own!

At this point you might be scratching your head or clicking your tongue thinking 'why the hell should I listen to you!?'

Well... I build bespoke, quirky vans with soul, and after building and living in three different vans in 2020 alone I’m definitely not some sort of prodigy-carpenter-come-master-builder, but I like to think I know more than the average person – or vanbuild virgin haphazardly spraying glue around before crafting a carpet cave. If you have trolled through Facebook or Instagram, you know the unimaginative head-to-toe, front-to-back "I dont have a clue what Im doing so I'll carpet everything" grey carpet scheme I’m talking about.

Sorry if you yourself belong to this demographic and are now hastily clicking away from my page, but its just not cool in a world where you can let your inner creative juices flow!

Anyway, I hope you find something of use to you, even if its just a laugh at my expense – enjoy!

What I do...

I take ugly duckling panel vans and turn them into beautiful, bespoke homes fit for a king. It’s that simple. I’ve always been obsessed with the romanticised dream of being able to live and travel (hopefully you are too if you’re on this page!). Before vans, I rode motorbikes from the age of 16. My last trip was across Australia in 2018 with nothing but a tent and all my crap stapled, glued and strapped to the bike. It was no longer functional. It was then that me and my partner at the time decided to buy a 4x4 and convert it. DAAAAAMNNN, I was hooked!

No more flapping freediving fins, panniers dropping off or melted backpacks from a sizzling exhaust and most importantly - freedom… Complete freedom (as long as you’ve got a bit of fuel). From there life has naturally gravitated towards renovating and living in vans when most other options are an overpriced, illogical and alien concept. Say no to a mortgage you’ll pay until you’re dead! Let your inner nomad out instead!
